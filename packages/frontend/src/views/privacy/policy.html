Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2025

1. Introduction

At PAKE-Man (“we”, “us”, “our”), protecting your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our services.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from visitors who browse our site or use our basic services. We only use technically necessary cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

3. User Accounts

When you create an account using Google sign-in, we temporarily store the following information to facilitate your experience:

  • Google email address
  • Full name
  • Profile picture URL

3.1 Data Deletion Upon Account Removal

If you choose to delete your account, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our databases, including your Google email address, full name, and profile picture URL. No residual backups or copies of this data will remain after deletion.

4. Cookies

We only use technically necessary cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. We do not use any tracking or analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share any personal data with third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
